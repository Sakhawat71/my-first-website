Prothom Alo

The Daily Prothom Alo is a major daily newspaper in Bangladesh, published from Dhaka, Bangladesh in the Bangla language. Prothom Alo is the second largest (based on circulation) newspaper in Bangladesh. According to National Media Survey 2014, conducted by MRB Bangladesh, more than 4.9 million people read the print edition of Prothom Alo every day. The online portal of Prothom Alo is the most visited Bangladeshi and Bengali website of the world. It started its journey on 4 November 1998.
